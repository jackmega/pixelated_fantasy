About Me

Hi, I'm Jack Smith. I have many hobbies including; archery, Dungeons and Dragons, board games, card games, video games, tae-kwon-do, and more. I have done archery since I was four, I shoot Olypmic Style Recurve and was a member of Junior Olympic Archery Development (JOAD) for a time. I have played Dungeons and Dragons since the fall of 2016; I prefer to be the Dungeon Master (DM), although I do like to play. I have played board and card games for as long as I can remember, my favorite board game is Monopoly. I love video games and built my own computer to play them, I have a GTX 1060 with 3GB of VRAM, 16GB of RAM, and a 1TB and a 2TB hard drives. I have done tae-kwon-do since I was eight, now I teach children tae-kwon-do for eight hours a week.

How I Record

At the moment I record with a Blue Yeti into Adobe Audition, which I use to record and edit the audio. In the future I would like to buy an Audio Technica AT2020, a Focusrite Scarlett Solo, and a AxcessAbles SF-101KIT (a microphone isolation shield). Eventually I am going to convert my closet inot a recording booth with acustic foam, a mini-ITX computer running outside, a monitor inside the closet, a wireless keyboard and mouse, and the Audio Technica AT2020 microphone.

As at the moment I don't have the greatest setup for recording I spend time in Adobe Audition to ensure that background noise does not leak into the audio; this background noise partly comes from my computer fans.

My Voice

My voice can be smooth, deep, and fun; I have a couple of accents at my disposal including British (BBC), British (London), British (Gruff London), and Irish. I am from the Western United States, so my natural voice has that style to it. I have not taken any voice acting or acting classes yet; I plan to take some in my second semester of college.

Work

If you would like me to do any voice over or voice acting for a project, please note that I am not interested in audio books, or radio work. I am willing to send you a raw (unedited) voice file, an edited voice file, a complete audio track (music included if supplied). I can send you these files through email, or if you have a recording studio nearby me (contact me if this is the case and we can attempt to work something out) I can come to the studio and record there.
